# welcher und was für ein — "which" vs "what kind of"

Both ask about a noun — *which* book? *what kind of* car? They look similar but differ in meaning and
declension.

> **welcher** = pick one from a known set ("which of these?").
> **was für ein** = describe the type ("what sort?").

---

## welcher — "which (one of them)"

Use **welch-** when the answer is **one specific item out of a known group**. It is a **der-word**: it
takes the **same endings as the definite article** ([[01 Bestimmte]] / der-words), matching the noun's
gender and case:

| | masc | fem | neut | plural |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| Nom | **welcher** | **welche** | **welches** | **welche** |
| Akk | **welchen** | welche | welches | welche |
| Dat | **welchem** | welcher | welchem | **welchen** |

> **Welcher** Zug fährt nach Köln? — Which train goes to Cologne? *(masc nom)*
> **Welches** Auto ist deins? — Which car is yours? *(neut nom)*
> In **welchem** Hotel wohnst du? — Which hotel are you staying in? *(dat after *in*)*

Endings match the *der/die/das* article table.

---

## was für ein — "what kind of"

Use **was für (ein)** to ask about the **type or quality**, not to single one out. Here **für is not a
preposition** and does not set the case. The case comes from the noun's role in the sentence, and only
the **ein** part declines (like the indefinite article, [[02 Unbestimmte]]):

> **Was für ein** Auto fährst du? — What kind of car do you drive? *(Auto = object, but ein takes Akk: *ein*)*
> **Was für eine** Musik magst du? — What kind of music do you like?
> **Was für einen** Mann sucht sie? — What kind of man is she looking for? *(masc Akk → einen)*

Plural / uncountable → drop the *ein*:

> **Was für** Bücher liest du? — What kind of books do you read?

---

## Contrast

> **Welches** Auto willst du? — *Which* car (of these) do you want? → expects: *das rote.*
> **Was für ein** Auto willst du? — *What kind of* car do you want? → expects: *ein schnelles.*

**welcher** narrows a known set; **was für ein** asks for a description.

---

## Summary

**welcher** = "which one" — a **der-word**, endings match the definite article. **was für ein** = "what
kind" — only **ein** declines (für does not set the case here). Choose by intent: select from a set
(welcher) vs describe a type (was für ein).
