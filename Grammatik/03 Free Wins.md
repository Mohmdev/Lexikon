## 3. Compounding â€” long words are just glued short ones

German builds big words by sticking small ones together, and the meaning is **literal**. Split them
and you can read them:

> **Hand**schuh = hand-shoe = **glove**
> **Kranken**haus = sick-house = **hospital**
> **Flug**hafen = flight-harbor = **airport**
> **Fern**seher = far-seer = **TV**

A scary 20-letter word is usually 3 words you already know in a trench coat. (The grammar of how
