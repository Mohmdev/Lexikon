# Partikeln

**Partikel** is German's class of **uninflectable words** — no endings, no case, no
gender. Most subtypes are covered elsewhere (degree words → [[Gradadverbien]], negation →
[[04 Negation]], infinitive *zu* → [[06 Infinitive Clauses]], yes/no answers → [[Interjektionen]]). Two
types are covered only here:

> **Modalpartikeln** color the tone of the whole sentence (*ja, doch, mal, denn, halt, wohl*).
> **Fokuspartikeln** spotlight one element of it (*auch, sogar, nur, erst*).

These are optional for **correctness** but essential for sounding **natural**.

## Summary

Particles are **uninflectable tone-words**. Learn the **Modalpartikeln** by function (*ja, doch, mal,
denn, halt, wohl* first) and the **Fokuspartikeln** by position, and use **stress + position** to tell
each from its homonym in other word classes.
